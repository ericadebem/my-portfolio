About Me

I was born in Brazil, but my family is Italian, so I grew up with a mix of cultures which made me a lively person, full of ideas!
This mix of cultures made me a very lively person, full of ideas!
My biggest hobby is listening to and telling stories, in all formats, be it music, books, chatting, or films.

After working in various fields, I became an entrepreneur in Brazil and had two companies that were my pride and joy for almost 10 years. Almost three years ago, I moved to Germany where I am learning German and English and have the opportunity to work with my passion: the field of technology.

In 2021, I took a Chatbot AI course that changed my professional future and opened up new opportunities all over the world. The process of continuous learning is crucial in the technology field, and being able to draw from my previous experiences is making this journey even more amazing.

In July 2022, I graduated in Experience Design (UX Design) and gained knowledge and practice in Interface Design (UI Design), advanced product management techniques, including UX Investigation, UX Writing, Experience Mapping, Usability, Prototyping, Workshop Facilitation, and Product Management. I also developed more Soft Skills relevant to the field. During this training, I learned about UX Friendly Frontend Development and used HTML5, CSS3, Materialize, and Bootstrap Frameworks.

In August 2022, I was selected as one of the 100 women for a backend scholarship at Empower Code for Women, a project of the Alura Include BR School and Zenvia BR Company. During this program, I learned and practiced more GIT & GITHUB | HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript | Scratch | Node.JS and was introduced to unit testing using Jest.
